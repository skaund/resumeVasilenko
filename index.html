Why did I choose this profession?

Creative work
Programming is undoubtedly a creative activity. Every day you will face different kinds of problems and find elegant and elegant solutions for them. Moreover, the higher your level as a professional, the more complex and non-standard goals they will set for you.
Demand
Today programming is one of the most in-demand specialties in the world, and the demand for it continues to grow. Social networks, websites, software for various purposes, Android and iOS applications, high-tech gadgets, self-learning neural networks - the list is endless.
Income
The programmer is one of the highest paid categories of workers, this profession is highly valued in the labor market. The salary of a specialist ranges from 30 to 300 thousand rubles. and higher, depending on the level of professional skills and knowledge.
Free schedule
Even if you work in an office, employers will most likely not require you to strictly adhere to the time limits of the working day. You can plan your time as it suits you, the main thing is to complete the tasks on time.
Professional Development
Coding is a profession in which self-education plays a big role. This is a constantly progressing and improving area, and in order to remain a competent specialist, it is necessary to devote a considerable part of the time to acquaintance with new developments and technologies.
Work for introverts
Of course, most likely, in the process of training and subsequent work, you will inevitably form your own circle of contacts with professional interests, where you can discuss all the issues that interest you. But directly in the process of work, there are few opportunities for communication, and you will spend a considerable part of the working day alone with yourself.
